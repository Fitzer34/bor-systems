Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 June 2026

HazardLink is a product of BOR Systems / HazardLink Ltd, a company based in Ireland. We take the privacy of your data seriously and handle it in line with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Irish Data Protection Acts.

1. Who we are

HazardLink Ltd ("we", "us") is the data controller for the personal data described below. For any privacy questions or to exercise your rights, contact privacy@hazardlink.ie.

2. What we collect

  • Account data — your name, work email address, role, and an optional mobile number (only if you choose to receive SMS alerts). Passwords are stored only as a secure cryptographic hash.
  • Operational data — the information your organisation creates while using the service: sites and buildings, floor plans you upload, device identifiers, and the sign/spill events and alert history generated by your hardware.
  • Technical data — IP address, browser or app version, and security/diagnostic logs, used to keep the service running and secure.

3. How we use it, and our legal basis

  • To provide and operate the service — performance of our contract with your organisation.
  • To send alerts and notifications, including push, email and (where you opt in) SMS — contract, and your consent for SMS.
  • To keep the service secure, prevent abuse, and improve our product — our legitimate interests.
  • To meet legal and regulatory obligations — legal obligation.

4. Who we share it with

We do not sell your data. We use a small number of trusted sub-processors to run the service, each bound by data-processing agreements:

  • Cloudflare — website and application hosting / content delivery.
  • Render — backend application and database hosting.
  • Twilio — SMS delivery (only the recipient number and message).
  • An email delivery provider for transactional email, and an error-monitoring service to detect faults.

5. International transfers

Some of our sub-processors may process data outside the European Economic Area. Where they do, the transfer is protected by appropriate safeguards such as the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses.

6. How long we keep it

We keep personal data for as long as your organisation has an active account, and then delete or anonymise it on request or after the account is closed, except where we must retain certain records to meet legal obligations.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, correct, erase, restrict, or port your data, to object to certain processing, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these, email privacy@hazardlink.ie. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Irish Data Protection Commission (dataprotection.ie).

8. How we protect it

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS), passwords are hashed, two-factor authentication is available on accounts, and access is restricted on a need-to-know basis.

9. Cookies

This marketing website uses no advertising or tracking cookies. When you sign in to the dashboard, a single login token is stored in your browser to keep you signed in — this is essential to the service and is not used for tracking.

10. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the latest version.

11. Contact

Questions? Email privacy@hazardlink.ie.

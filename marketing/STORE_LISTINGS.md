## Full description (Apple 4000 chars / Google 4000 chars)

```
HazardLink turns ordinary wet-floor signs into connected safety sensors.
The moment one is lifted off its mount, your cleaning team gets a push
notification, the dashboard goes red, and the response is logged — every
step, with timestamps and photo evidence, so nothing slips through the cracks.

—— Why HazardLink ——

• REAL-TIME ALERTS — Phone, Apple Watch / Wear OS, Slack and Teams. You
  always know when a spill happens.

• AIRTAG-STYLE FIND — Ultra-Wideband precision finding (UWB) walks your
  cleaners straight to the lifted sign in any building. cm-level
  accuracy on iPhone 11+, Pixel 6 Pro+, Galaxy S21 Ultra+.

• REPORTS & RECORDS — Auto-generated reports document every spill,
  response time, and resolution. A complete, exportable safety record
  for your own health-and-safety reviews and accountability.

• PHOTO PROOF — Cleaners snap a quick photo of the cleaned area before
  closing each alert. Tamper-resistant audit trail.

• MULTI-LANGUAGE — Notifications in English, Polish, Portuguese,
  Spanish, Romanian and Lithuanian. Your cleaning team understands
  alerts in their native language.

• ANTI-THEFT — Signs that go missing for 24h auto-alert your admin
  team. Save €300–600 per year in replacement costs.

• HEAT-MAP ANALYTICS — Discover which zones are repeat offenders.
  Fix the root cause, not just the symptom.

• AFTER-HOURS MODE — Different escalation policy at night. Alerts
  route to your security guard rather than waking the cleaning team.

• SLACK + TEAMS — Post alerts straight into your existing channel.

—— How it works ——

1. We supply small hangers that mount on the wall (one per sign).
2. You hang your own existing wet-floor signs on them.
3. A magnet inside each sign keeps the hanger "armed".
4. The moment a cleaner lifts the sign, the magnetic field changes
   and a sub-€1 Hall sensor fires the alert.
5. The alert reaches phones, watches, and the web dashboard in
   under 5 seconds.

—— Who it's for ——

• Commercial cleaning companies managing multiple client sites
• Facility managers in offices, retail, hospitality, healthcare
• Health &amp; safety officers responsible for slip/trip prevention
• Any business where a sudden spill needs a cleaning response

—— Hardware ——

Hangers run on a single rechargeable 21700 lithium cell — one charge
per year. Sign tags use replaceable CR2032 cells (2-year life). Gateway
is a small Wi-Fi puck (one per building, USB-C powered).

—— Open standards ——

LoRa for sign-to-gateway communication. BLE for first-time Wi-Fi setup.
UWB for precision finding. Multi-tenant cloud with end-to-end HMAC.
Sentry monitoring. Public status page.

Questions? hello@hazardlink.ie
```

## Privacy nutrition (Apple — what your app collects)

Data linked to user identity:
- Contact Info: email address (for account)
- Identifiers: device ID (for push notifications)
- Diagnostics: crash data (Sentry, no PII)

Data NOT linked to user identity:
- Usage data: alert response times (aggregated for analytics)

Data NOT collected:
- Location, photos (other than user-uploaded resolution photos), audio,
  contacts, browsing history, advertising data.
